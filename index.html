Snacks

snacks that cater to specific dietary preferences. The best part is that all of their snacks are guilt-free, so you can munch away without any guilt or regrets. I highly recommend Snackible for anyone who is looking for healthier snack options.

 

Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori chicken has a warm flavor from spices like garam masala, turmeric, ginger, and cayenne pepper. This easy marinade for tandoori chicken is made with simple ingredients: Full-fat Greek yogurt – The main component of tandoori chicken marinade is yogurt!

 

Chicken tikka

Chicken tikka is a chicken dish originating in the Indian subcontinent during the Mughal era, the dish is popular in India

 

Paneer tikka

Paneer Tikka Masala can literally be translated to grilled cottage cheese in a spiced gravy!

 

Chicken garlic tikka

It is a spicy, sweet, and slightly sour crispy appetizer made with chicken, garlic tikka.

 

Hara bhara Kabab

Hara Bhara Kabab are popular North Indian snack patties made with plenty of leafy greens and vegetables like spinach, coriander leaves, green peas, potatoes and spices. The name hara bhara kabab means kababs filled with greens.

 

Tandoori roti

Tandoori Roti, mix maida, whole wheat flour, curd, salt, oil, sugar and baking powder in a bowl. Add water slowly and make a tight dough.

 

Tandoori butter roti

tandoori butter roti we have used curd instead of yeast as it helps in fermentation of the dough. Sugar is add which helps in fermentation and also to balance out the flavour, gives beautiful colour to the rotis.

 

Lachha paratha

Lachha paratha is quite appetizing to look at, and even more interesting to bite into, because the layers separated by ghee give a crumbly, melt-in-the-mouth texture.

 

Butter naan

Naan is an Indian bread which is on every restaurant menu. It is a rich Indian bread that goes well with most Indian gravies. Everyone loves the soft texture of a good Butter naan.

 

Aalu paratha

Aloo Paratha are popular Indian flatbreads stuffed with a delicious spiced potato mixture. In Hindi, Aloo means “Potatoes” and Paratha means “Layered Flatbreads”.

 

Paneer paratha

Paneer paratha is a popular North Indian flatbread made with whole wheat flour dough and stuffed with savory, spiced, grated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) stuffing.

 

Garlic naan

Mostly prepared during parties and celebrations, the list of curries to pair with garlic naan are endless. However, it pairs well with Malai Kofta, Butter Chicken, Shahi Paneer, and Dal Makhni.
