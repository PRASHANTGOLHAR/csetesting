    Industrial Visit

    The R&D Cell of the Department is all set to motivate and encourage the faculty and the students to carry out research work, generate project proposals, to tap funds from governmental and industrial funding agencies and establish Centre of Excellence in the college as well as to interact with industries. The Cell facilitates the faculty to enhance their academic qualification and to help them in proper planning and coordination of the research and development activities.

    The Cell periodically reviews the research work carried out by the faculty, while emphasizing the importance of industryâ€“institute interaction, and facilitates taking up project and students work in Live and Research Projects, which in turn, helps students in their training and placement.
